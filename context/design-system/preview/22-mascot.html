Sticker-pack rule. Mascots are 32–68px decorations that punctuate content — next to a headline word, inside a badge, in a section corner, in the margin. Never use them as a hero centerpiece, large standalone illustration, or product photo replacement. Max two stickers per page; pick the emotion that maps to the section's intent (waving = hello, star-burst = reviews, thinking = FAQ, money = pricing, etc.).
