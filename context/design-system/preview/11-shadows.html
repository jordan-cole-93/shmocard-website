Shadows — three values, strict rules
Decide which one to use:
  1. Is it the one primary CTA or a hard hero module? → chunk (4px)
  2. Is it small + interactive on a busy surface? → chunk-sm (2px)
  3. Anything else (cards, rows, panels, body content) → none
Soft card
none --shadow-none Default for 95% of surfaces. Soft cards, product cards, pack rows, FAQ items, callouts, image frames, all body content.
Add
chunk-sm --shadow-chunk-sm Small interactive, needs to read as tappable. Secondary buttons, qty stepper, upsell "+" circle. Always pairs with --outline-hard.
Buy now
chunk --shadow-chunk Reserved. The one primary CTA per section, and .shm-card--hard spotlight modules (hero, "most popular" pack, final CTA). Never on body content.
Smell test. If every CTA on the page has chunk, none of them are special. Cap at 2 chunky shadows per page — one primary CTA + one hard module is plenty.
