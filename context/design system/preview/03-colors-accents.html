Section text & accent rules

Each section color has its own text + accent palette. Use the rules below — never improvise.

Marshmallow #FFFBF1 · 60%

Real reviews. Real customers.

Body copy reads in ink-3 for a softer hierarchy on light surfaces.

CTA · ember 2nd · chocolate ghost · cocoa
  • Heading cocoa-deep · accent ember · body ink-3
  • Buttons: primary ember, secondary chocolate, tertiary cocoa-outline
Graham soft #FFE0C2 · 25%

Built for local crews.

Body shifts to cocoa-deep at 78% — graham soft is too warm for ink-3.

CTA · ember 2nd · chocolate
  • Heading cocoa-deep · accent ember · body cocoa-deep / 78%
  • Same button stack as marshmallow — graham reads as a warm marshmallow
Ember #FF5B1F · 10%

Loved by 300+ shops.

Body is white at 92%. Honey is the only accent — never use ember on ember.

CTA · cream alt · honey
  • Heading marshmallow · accent honey · body marshmallow / 92%
  • Buttons flip to cream (primary) or honey (alt) — ember on ember disappears
Cocoa-deep #3B1F14 · 5%

Tap, post, get 5 stars.

Body is marshmallow at 78%. The closer CTA — confident, final.

CTA · ember alt · honey
  • Heading marshmallow · accent honey · body marshmallow / 78%
  • Ember CTA pops on cocoa — primary stays ember here, unlike on ember section

The auto-flip rule. The italic accent (now any inline highlight) flips automatically: .shm-bg-cocoa and .shm-bg-chocolate wrap any em or .shm-italic in honey. On marshmallow / graham / ember sections, accents stay ember (or flip to honey on ember itself).
