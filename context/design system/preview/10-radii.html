Corner radii — five values, decision rules
10
sm --radius-sm · 10px Pack rows, sub-brand glyph tiles, family tiles, tight icon containers.
16
md --radius-md · 16px Inline interactive surfaces — FAQ items, callout strips, product thumbs.
22
lg --radius-lg · 22px Default card radius — product cards, soft/hard cards, content panels.
28
xl --radius-xl · 28px Hero modules, gallery main image, large image frames.
∞
full --radius-full · 999px Anything interactive that should look like a pill — buttons, badges, eyebrows, inputs, qty steppers.

Decision rule. Interactive = pill. Inline surface (row, item, strip) = md. Card or panel = lg. Hero or large image = xl. Glyph tile = sm. No xs, no 2xl.
