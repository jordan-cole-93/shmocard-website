# context.md — What Is This Project

## What this site is

Brand website for Shmocard at `shmocard.com`. Retail front door — not an ad funnel. Traffic comes from organic search, direct, referral, and existing customers.

## Core thesis

**One card per employee, not one per shop.**

- One card behind the counter = ~2 reviews/week
- One card per employee = ~15 reviews/week (~7.5× more)

Every section of the site returns to this bulk math argument. Locked homepage headline: **"Built for crews. Priced for bulk."**

## Brand

- **Mascot:** The S'more character — not a logo, a narrative carrier. Appears throughout the site.
- **Palette:** S'more-derived warm earth tones — Cream & Snow (surfaces), Ember & Peach (brand accent), Honey (pop accent), Chocolate & Ink (depth).
- **Style:** Warm Professional — refined, sans-dominant with serif italic accents. Like Linear or Stripe but with S'more warmth. Hairlines and soft shadows, not thick borders.
- **Voice:** Plainspoken, warm, confident. Tradesperson test: would a barber or pawnshop owner feel respected?
