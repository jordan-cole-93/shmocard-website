Section color rotation

Four section surfaces, used as a rhythm — never random. One per <section>.

DEFAULT · 60%

Marshmallow

Hero, copy-heavy sections, anything that should feel calm and breathable.

SECONDARY · 25%

Graham soft

Set-piece sections — features, lifestyle rows. Warmer than marsh, still light.

SPICE · 10%

Ember

High-energy moment. Once per page max — CTA or "this is the deal".

ANCHOR · 5%

Cocoa deep

Quiet anchor — testimonials climax, FAQ, footer. Once per page max.

Default page rotation
1 · HeroMarshmallow
2 · FeaturesGraham soft
3 · Use casesMarshmallow
4 · Social proofEmber · spice
5 · FAQMarshmallow
6 · Final CTACocoa deep · anchor
Rules
  1. Adjacent sections must contrast — never two of the same color in a row.
  2. Ember and Cocoa-deep are spice — at most one of each per page.
  3. Wave divider between every section, color-matched to the next.
  4. On Ember and Cocoa-deep, headings flip to marshmallow and accents flip to honey (handled automatically by .shm-bg-ember and .shm-bg-cocoa).
